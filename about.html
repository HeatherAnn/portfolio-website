MY NAME IS HEATHER BISAILLION AND I'M OBSESSED WITH ART.
Usually drinking coffee and carrying around a bag full of art supplies, I live for creativity. I grew up in Ottawa and went to a school in the middle of no-where. Seriously, there was a field of haybails right next to us. Needless to say, it wasn't particularily artistic. In fact, an artist from where I'm from was the minority. Regardless I purused what I loved to do, which was draw until about 3am (probably when the cows next to my school wake up). Now my love for creativity has expanded into a career and my own style. I'm inspired by fine art and tattoos that I love to evolve into a professional creative piece.
